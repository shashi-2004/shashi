Elizabeth Wanjiru Wathuti

In high school, Wathuti established an environmental club and organized tree planting activities. She also began to speak out about the importance of protecting forests and addressing climate change. After graduating from high school, she enrolled at Kenyatta University, where she studied environmental studies and community development.

The initiative also educates young people about the importance of environmental conservation and climate action.
